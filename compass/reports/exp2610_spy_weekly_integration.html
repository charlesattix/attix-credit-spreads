Capacity (from EXP-2580)

EXP-2580 reported spy_wk capacity at 20% portfolio weight:

  • Soft cap: $1.5 B of stream notional / $7.6 B portfolio AUM
  • Hard cap: $7.6 B of stream notional / $37.9 B portfolio AUM
  • SLV calendar (replaced): $16 M soft / $82 M hard — roughly 500× smaller.

Recommendation

  • Baseline (7-stream, SLV included): net Sharpe 4.53, net CAGR 74.37%, max DD 5.46%, drag 2221 bps. AUM ceiling ~$82M (SLV hard cap).
  • Add spy_wk as 8th stream: net Sharpe 3.54 (Δ -0.99), net CAGR 41.53%, drag 2408 bps. AUM ceiling still $82M (SLV still in).
  • Swap slv_cal → spy_wk: net Sharpe 3.97 (Δ -0.56), net CAGR 40.29%, drag 1710 bps (saves 510 bps vs baseline). AUM ceiling jumps from $82M to $7.6B — the big win.

DO NOT SWAP slv_cal → spy_wk. Net Sharpe drops by 0.56, more than the 0.5 tolerance. SLV stays in the portfolio; the AUM ceiling remains $82M until a different replacement is found.

Methodology & honest caveats

  • Trade generator: imported verbatim from compass.exp2580_spy_weekly_cs.run_weekly_trades. Same 10-DTE target, 3% OTM short, $5 wide, 50% profit, 2× stop, VIX<40 framework EXP-2580 validated.
  • Cube build: EXP-2450 sparse 7-stream cube with spy_wk added as an 8th column or substituted for slv_cal. Same exit-date attribution, no smearing.
  • Walk-forward engine: EXP-2400 walk_forward_combined called verbatim — Ledoit-Wolf covariance + risk parity + 15% vol target + 3% DD circuit breaker. No per-stream re-tuning.
  • Cost drag for each variant: sum of per-stream bps from EXP-2420's real-IronVault cost model. For the swap (slv_cal → spy_wk) we SUBTRACT the slv_cal line (1200 bps) and ADD the spy_wk estimated line. For the add variant we only add spy_wk. Drag is applied via EXP-2420's net_sharpe_from_drag.
  • spy_wk cost scaling is linear in (trades × legs × contracts) relative to EXP-2420's exp1220 reference line. This is a first-order approximation; the real slippage has a √(notional/ADV) component which would slightly reduce spy_wk cost at similar notional. Honest-bias: my estimate is conservative.
  • The swap test is an AUM-scaling proposition. Even if the net Sharpe is roughly flat, moving the 20% SLV weight into spy_wk unlocks a ~500× capacity increase, which is the whole point.
