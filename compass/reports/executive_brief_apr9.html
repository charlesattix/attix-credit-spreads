2. Three Concrete Next Steps

These are the actionable items ranked by impact per unit of effort. Items 1–2 come from tc_gap_analysis.json; item 3 comes from execution_timing_analysis.json. Baseline net Sharpe is 6.39 (v8a + VIX ladder).

1

Selective trade frequency compression (v5_hedge + slv_cal)

Reduce the trade cadence on v5_hedge (tail hedge) and slv_cal (futures roll harvest). v5_hedge is the largest single DD driver across the walk-forward (6 of 20 fold DDs) because it bleeds in calm periods by design. Compressing its turnover cuts commission drag without touching the hedge payoff. slv_cal is a small-weight sleeve that pays too much in commissions relative to its alpha contribution.
Impact: +0.40 Sharpe (biggest single lever) Effort: ~1 day — parameter edit in compass/crisis_alpha_v5.py and compass/exp1770_commodity_calendars.py Risk: Low — no broker change, no execution wiring, reversible via config
Source: tc_gap_analysis.json lever A. Combines EXP-2560 v5_hedge noise-reduction finding with slv_cal commission savings.
2

Midpoint limit orders + TWAP splitting (Alpaca-compatible subset)

Replace market orders with midpoint limits and split larger sleeves across 2–3 slices via a TWAP schedule. This captures ~60% of the full CBOE AIM auction savings (EXP-2470) without requiring a broker change to IBKR. Midpoint limits are natively supported by Alpaca; TWAP splitting is a scheduler-layer change in compass.exp2690_signal_generators and the Alpaca connector.
Impact: +0.075 Sharpe Effort: ~2–3 days — scheduler + order-router changes Risk: Medium — needs paper validation of fill rates before live
Source: tc_gap_analysis.json lever B. Does not touch the backtest. Fill rate must be monitored (EXP-2670 gate G6: ≥50% limit-at-mid fill rate).
3

Time-of-day execution: avoid 09:30, target 12:55 window

The intraday spread analysis shows SPY bid-ask is 3.5× wider at 09:30 (16.1 bps) than at 12:55 (4.6 bps). Shifting all scheduled entries from the open to the midday window saves ~109 bps/yr on SPY and a total of ~121 bps/yr across the portfolio (SPY+QQQ+XLF+XLI+GLD+SLV weighted). Implementation is a one-line schedule change in the cron driver.
Impact: +0.033 Sharpe (≈121 bps/yr savings) Effort: ~2 hours — change scan_time: "09:35" to "12:55" in the master config Risk: Very low — causal, reversible, zero infra change
Source: execution_timing_analysis.json. Best bar time 12:55 (4.6 bps), worst 09:30 (16.1 bps). Per-ticker weighted savings sum to 121.5 bps/yr.
