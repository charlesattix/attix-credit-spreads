7. Next Steps — Wave 8 Plan

With Sharpe 6.0 met in backtest, the next wave pivots from alpha discovery to validation and deployment:

  1. Paper trade the Path A config (C2_max_sharpe + V+F) for 8 weeks per MASTERPLAN Phase 10. Target: paper P&L within ±30% of backtest expectation.
  2. Tighten EXP-2070 VIX term structure overlay integration: run the stacked V+F+T variant through EXP-2050 and measure whether it lifts Sharpe above 7.0 or just adds complexity.
  3. Kill the stale EXP-1870 stress-test audit (built on pre-utilization-fix data) and rebuild on the EXP-2050 config. The DD estimate must match the live harness to ±1%.
  4. GLD/QQQ backfill (per MASTERPLAN data inventory) to unblock the cross-asset pairs that couldn't run this wave.
  5. Re-audit the Sharpe 6.96 claim against EXP-1890 Portfolio Risk Manager: run the 5-stream allocation through `make_decision()` daily and confirm the RiskDecision output never enters WARN or HALT during the 2020-2025 replay.
  6. Lightweight ensemble of Marginal-grade signals (VVIX, put-skew, 1DTE decay, carry): they each missed the +0.3 solo bar but may combine usefully. Build as EXP-2100.
  7. Seed $25K at 1× after paper window, scale only after 4+ weeks of real-money results matching paper within ±15%.
Risk caveats for Carlos to weigh before live deployment:
  • The Path A result uses optimised weights over the full 2020-2025 window. Out-of-sample robustness on 2026 data is not yet measured.
  • V+F overlay's contribution is concentrated on ~30% of trading days (FOMC + high-VoV weeks). A regime in which those filters stop discriminating would collapse the alpha.
  • The v5_hedge stream drags −1.03% CAGR standalone. Its hedge value is real but conditional on systemic stress events which did not occur in EXP-2080's correlation detector window.
  • Cross-vol-arb (EXP-2020) has only 271 trades over 6 years; statistical confidence is weaker than EXP-1220's 171-trade record.
