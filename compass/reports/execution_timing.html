Methodology & Caveats

  • Friction proxy, not real bid/ask. (H−L)/C correlates with bid-ask but overstates it during fast-moving periods. Real NBBO would confirm these patterns more precisely — available in Polygon Options Advanced (EXP-2930 scaling plan).
  • SLV data gap. Zero SLV contracts in IronVault — reported honestly, not simulated. EXP-2930 recommends Polygon Starter ($199/mo) to backfill this plus IWM and CBOE VIX calls.
  • Only SPY has intraday bars. QQQ/GLD/XLF/XLI intraday analysis would need additional data subscriptions. The day-of-week analysis for those tickers is still fully real.
  • Calibration anchor is a choice. We anchor LOW regime to 5 bps (Almgren-Chriss). Institutional execution might reach 2-3 bps in LOW; retail execution might be 8-12 bps. The RELATIVE structure (DoW, intraday, regime) holds regardless of the absolute anchor.
  • 50 round-trips/yr is the v8a baseline. Higher turnover strategies compound the savings linearly.
