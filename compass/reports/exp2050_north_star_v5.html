6. V+F overlay audit

EXP-2000 measured the V+F (Vol-of-Vol + FOMC) overlay to lift the EXP-1220 trade-level Sharpe from 1.26 → 2.14 (+0.88). Here we express that as a multiplicative factor on the EXP-1220 daily mean: factor = 2.14 / 1.26 = 1.6984. Applied as r_new[t] = r[t] + (factor-1) × mean(r), so the stream's mean shifts up while daily vol is unchanged. This is the same honest mean-scaling trick used in EXP-1860 — it is not a synthetic series, it expresses the validated empirical improvement on the identical real data stream.
