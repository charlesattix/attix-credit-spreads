Interpretation

  • slow_grind_bear: measures whether a steady 2%/month equity drift trips the circuit breaker. Vol-targeting will de-lever into the drift; the breaker fires on cumulative drawdown, not on velocity.
  • flash_crash_v: tests whether the 3%/6% breaker catches a single-day gap-down before it compounds. Hedge sleeve (v5_hedge) absorbs some of the impact.
  • stagflation: zero-mean + 1.5× vol for 2 years. The LW risk-parity re-fit every 63 days should reduce weights on the equity-sensitive streams as their realised vol spikes.
  • correlation_breakdown: all streams converge to ρ≈0.8. The effective N drops from 6.69 → ~1, and the cube's diversification benefit collapses. Tests whether LW shrinkage catches the regime change fast enough to de-lever.

Scope note

These numbers are a forward-looking characterisation of the allocator + breaker + vol targeting machinery, not a claim about historical performance. Real stress historically has looked like COVID 2020 and 2022 — captured in the baseline. This experiment is about regimes outside that sample.
