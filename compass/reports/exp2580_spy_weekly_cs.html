Method

  • Entry: every Monday (or next trading day), pick SPY put expiration with 6-14 day DTE. Enter 3% OTM short put, 5-point width.

  • Sizing: 3% of $100K capital as max loss → 1-4 contracts.
  • Correlation: convert weekly trades to exit-date daily return series on the cached EXP-2280 sparse index, correlate against exp1220 column.
  • Capacity: 1% ADV soft / 5% ADV hard × 20% assumed portfolio weight.
