Method

  • SPY P/C = SUM(put volume) / SUM(call volume) per day across all SPY contracts in IronVault.

  • Entry gate blocks: PCR percentile rank below 25% (60-day window), VIX/VIX3M > 1.0, or put_volume z-score > 2.0.
  • Sizing modifier: contracts × 1.3 when PCR percentile rank ≥ 75%, × 0.5 when ≤ 25% (else 1.0).
