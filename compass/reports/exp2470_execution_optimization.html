Technique catalogue

KeyTechniqueBid-askSlippageCommMechanism
ALimit at mid (50% fill rate)×0.50×1.00×1.00Limit orders at the midprice fill roughly half the time; unfilled orders are retried as market orders at the full spread. Net effective bid-ask cost is halved.
BPatient execution (pre-close window)×1.00×0.75×1.00Execute inside the 15-min pre-close window instead of the opening minutes. End-of-day option ADV is ~2x the open, which compresses √-impact by ~25%.
CRoute reallocation to XLI/XLF×0.78×1.05×1.00EXP-2420 per-$10K round-trip cost ranks XLI $0.77 < XLF $1.48 ≈ SPY $1.50. Shifting 40% of SPY-heavy and 30% of GLD-heavy notional into XLI/XLF blends bid-ask × 0.78. Small 5% slippage penalty from XLI's thinner ADV.
DMulti-leg combo orders×0.75×1.00×1.00Submit verticals and calendars as single combo orders. Price improvement on combo fills is typically 20-30% of spread. Applied to credit-spread + calendar streams only.

Method

  • Source: compass/reports/exp2420_transaction_costs.json (real IronVault + Yahoo cost model).
  • Each technique applied as a multiplicative factor on the relevant cost line.
  • Techniques stack multiplicatively: final_bid_ask = baseline × ∏ ba_factor_i.
  • Net Sharpe recomputed from gross 5.96 (ann vol 15.12%) minus drag/vol.
  • Commission line is held fixed — no execution technique reduces Alpaca fees. Trade-frequency reduction (Phase 8) is the only lever there.
