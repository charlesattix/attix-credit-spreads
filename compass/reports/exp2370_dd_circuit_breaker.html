Method

  • Cached sparse 7-stream frame from EXP-2200 build_streams.
  • Walk-forward 252 train / 63 test / step 63 → 20 folds (same as EXP-2280).
  • Per fold: equal_risk weights + 15% vol target (scale = 0.15 / σ_train × √252).
  • Causal circuit: on day t, compute trailing-DD over days [t-20 .. t-1] from raw scaled portfolio returns. If DD > threshold → leverage scaled to 0× (flatten) or 1× (reduce); else base 1.0× (= already vol-targeted).
  • Thresholds tested: 3%, 5%, 8%, 10% × modes flatten / reduce = 8 configs.
  • Trip rate = fraction of OOS days where the circuit was engaged.
