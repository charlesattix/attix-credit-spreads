Methodology & caveats

  • Spread: log(a) − log(b) on every unordered pair from {USO, UNG, GLD, DBA}.
  • Signal: 60-day z-score of the log ratio.
  • Rule: mean reversion. Enter at |z|>z_entry, exit at |z|
  • Walk-forward: 252d train / 63d OOS / 63d step.
  • Data: Yahoo Finance daily auto-adjusted close, 2020-01-01→2025-12-31. Real prices, no synthetics.
  • Caveats: ETF ratio mean reversion is not a true calendar spread — it is a relative-value pair trade. Two ETFs tracking different commodities can drift apart for years (regime change), so this should be combined with a regime filter or hard stop before any production use.
  • Correlation vs EXP-1220: yearly only (n≤6). Directional, not statistically significant.
