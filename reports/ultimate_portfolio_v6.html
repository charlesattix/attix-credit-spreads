3. Architecture

  • Dynamic leverage: 3-ramp product (VIX, term structure, realized vol). Target 2.0x in calm, 0.3x floor. EMA smoothing (3-day halflife).
  • Collar hedge: Sell 3% OTM calls to fund 5% OTM puts. Real IronVault put costs (1.8-7.2%/yr depending on VIX). Call income offsets ~70%. Net cost ~0.2-2.2%/yr.
  • DD brake: DD > 5% forces leverage below 0.9x. DD > 8% forces below 0.5x.
  • COVID response: VIX spike to 58 → leverage drops from 2.0x to ~0.3x in 5 days. Put payoffs compensate for the crash. Portfolio recovers within weeks.
