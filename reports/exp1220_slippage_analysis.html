Slippage Model Details

1. Bid-Ask Spread Model

Base: $0.03/leg for ATM SPY options at VIX=20, 30 DTE. Scales with:

  • DTE factor: 0.8x (7-14d) to 1.5x (<5d)
  • OTM factor: 1.0x + moneyness×5 (5% OTM → 1.25x)
  • VIX factor: 0.8x (VIX<15) to 5.0x (VIX>50)
  • Round-trip: 4 half-spread crossings (entry + exit × 2 legs)

2. Market Impact (Almgren-Chriss)

impact = κ × σ_daily × price × √(participation_rate)

  • κ = 0.3 (empirical impact coefficient for SPY options)
  • σ_daily = VIX / √252
  • participation = contracts_per_trade / 3M daily SPY option volume

3. Fill Probability

Market orders: 100% fill. Limit orders: 95% base, decaying with participation.

  • $1M: ~95% limit fill
  • $100M: ~92% limit fill
  • Unfilled limit orders assumed to be filled at market (adds ~1-2 bps)
