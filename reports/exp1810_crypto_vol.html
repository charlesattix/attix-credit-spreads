Honest verdict

Confidence: LOW (16 months of data, no historical chains).

  • IBIT measured IV today: 54.1% vs break-even 15.1% → edge +39.0 pp
  • SPY measured IV today: 22.4% vs break-even 0.8% → edge +21.6 pp
  • Tail risk: IBIT realized vol is dramatically higher than SPY (~3-4x). The break-even IV reflects this.
  • Without historical option chains, we cannot fully validate that crypto VRP is positive over time. The current snapshot is a single point in time. EXP-600 paper trading is the only path to honest PnL data.
