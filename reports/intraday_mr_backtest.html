Hypothesis: When SPY gaps materially away from the previous close (>0.5%), the gap tends to partially close during the trading session.

Entry logic: If gap down > 0.5% at open → BUY at open, SELL at close. If gap up > 0.5% → SHORT at open, COVER at close. Skip gaps > 5% (news events).

Edge type: Statistical mean reversion. Different from credit spread theta (EXP-1220) and trend following (EXP-1780). Should provide diversification.
