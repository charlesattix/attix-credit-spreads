🚨 Reality Check on These Numbers
  • These are theoretical backtested returns. Real-world slippage, execution delays, and market impact will reduce them significantly — especially at higher leverage and larger account sizes.
  • Market impact: At 4-5x, you're running many concurrent positions. Fill quality degrades. The bigger the account, the worse the fills.
  • Capacity ceiling: SPY options liquidity caps realistic AUM at ~$50M for this strategy. At 4-5x with large accounts, you'll move the market.
  • Compounding assumption: These assume perfect reinvestment every cycle. Real trading has cash drag.
  • One bad week at 5x = 30% gone. Psychologically, very few people can hold through that without panic-closing.

  1. Paper trade 2x and 3x side by side for 8 weeks
  2. Go live at 2x — prove it works with real money and real emotions
  3. After 3 months of live 2x, if DD never exceeded 10%, scale to 3x
  4. Keep a "degen account" with 5-10% of capital at 4x or 5x if you want the rush
  5. Add Crisis Alpha hedge (EXP-1780) to reduce DD ~30% at any leverage level
🛡️ Why Credit Spreads Handle High Leverage
  • Defined risk per trade: Max loss = spread width. No margin call surprises.
  • 91% win rate: Losses are rare and capped.
  • Leverage = concurrent positions, not margin borrowing. Fundamentally different from leveraged stocks.
  • But: Multiple positions can all lose in the same week during crashes → that's where the DD comes from.
