4. How It Works

  • Dynamic leverage: 3-ramp product (VIX, term structure, realized vol). Target 1.8x in calm, 0.2x floor in crisis.
  • Tail risk hedge: SPY puts (60%) + VIX calls (40%) at 2% annual cost budget. Puts pay 12x on down days, VIX calls pay 20x on spikes.
  • Integration: When hedge is active, leverage can stay HIGHER (hedge covers downside). When hedge is off, conservative dynamic scaling applies.
  • Emergency brake: DD >5% forces leverage below 0.8x. DD >8% forces below 0.4x.
  • Smoothing: EMA with 3-day halflife prevents whipsaw.
