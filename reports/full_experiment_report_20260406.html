Methodology Notes

  • Sharpe formula: arithmetic mean of daily excess returns / std × sqrt(252). NOT (CAGR - rf) / vol, which overstates by 1.2-2.4x at high return levels.
  • Walk-forward: expanding window where possible, with IS = pre-2023 and OOS = 2023-2025 for strategies with sufficient data.
  • Transaction costs: Commissions included ($0.65/leg). Bid-ask implicit in IronVault close prices.
  • Correlation: Yearly where sample is small; monthly where possible (EXP-1820).
  • Capacity: Max AUM = 5% of ATM ADV participation per trade.
