Risk Layer Specification

Layer 1: Dynamic Leverage (Regime-Aware 0.3x–1.8x)

Leverage = target × vix_scale × ts_scale × rvol_scale, EMA-smoothed (halflife=1.3135d).

VIX ZoneThresholdLeverage Scale
Calm< 151.0x (full)

Elevated< 28~0.4x

Also scales on VIX/VIX3M term structure (contango=full, deep inversion=min) and 20-day realized vol.

Layer 2: Tail Risk Hedging (SPY Puts + VIX Calls)

Annual budget: 2% of portfolio. Allocates 60% to SPY puts, 40% to VIX calls. Budget scales up to 3x in crisis (crisis_score=1.0). Hedge ratio: 30% normal, 80% crisis.

  • SPY puts: 20-delta OTM puts. Payoff triggers on SPY drops > 0.5%. 12x convex multiplier.
  • VIX calls: Payoff triggers on VIX spikes > 5%. 20x convex multiplier for tail events.
  • Crisis score: Composite of VIX (30%), term structure (20%), DD (25%), realized vol (10%), momentum (15%).

Layer 3: Event Gates (FOMC / CPI / NFP)

Automatic position scaling around macro events:

Multiple events: per-type minimum applied (not multiplicative).

Layer 4: Position-Level Stop Losses

  • Fixed stop: Exit if single-day loss exceeds 3%
  • Trailing stop: Exit if drawdown from position peak exceeds 5%
  • Position tracking resets every 14 days (rolling trade windows)

Layer 5: Portfolio DD Circuit Breaker

  • Trigger: Portfolio drawdown hits 10% → cut leverage to 0.5x
  • Recovery: DD must recover below 5% before resuming normal leverage
  • Prevents catastrophic ruin during extended drawdowns
  • Hysteresis gap (10% on / 5% off) prevents whipsaw
