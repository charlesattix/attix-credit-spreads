Audit Methodology

Each experiment is checked for 7 integrity issues discovered during the project:

  1. Dilution — >50% zero-return days inflate Sharpe by reducing measured vol
  2. Synthetic Data — np.random, Black-Scholes, or heuristic pricing instead of real market data
  3. Look-Ahead Bias — future data used in entry/exit decisions
  4. Sharpe Formula — must use arithmetic returns, not CAGR-based (which inflates 1.07-2.4×)
  5. Survivorship Bias — expired/delisted options properly handled
  6. Transaction Costs — bid-ask spreads + commissions included
  7. Capacity — trades within <5% of ATM daily volume

Grading: A = all pass, B+ = 1 warning, B = 2 warnings, C = 1 fail, D = 2+ fails, F = any critical.
