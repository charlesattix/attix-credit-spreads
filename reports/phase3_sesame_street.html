Today's Big Takeaways
What Did We Learn Today?
✅
Use Ensemble, not XGBoost alone
Three friends working together > one friend. It wins on AUC every single year and doesn't panic when markets get scary. It takes 6 seconds instead of 0.2 seconds — but that's a tiny price for a much better helper.
🚫
Don't use ML to pick credit spread trades
The cookie jar is already 84% good cookies. Both models just say "eat everything." Neither can tell the bad 16% apart. The strategy rules already do the picking — ML can't improve on that.
🚦
Use ML as a traffic light for CS instead
Green light: Bull market? Trade normally, maybe trade MORE (scale 3→7 contracts). Red light: Bear market detected? STOP trading credit spreads until it's sunny again. This would've saved 2022.
🌧️
ML earns its keep when the weather is bad
Sunny bull markets? Both models work. Stormy, scary markets? That's where Ensemble is worth its weight in gold. Build the system to lean on ML MORE when things get rough.
🍪
For mixed strategies, ML filtering actually works!
When you include straddles and iron condors (riskier cookies), the Ensemble lifts win rate from 59.5% to 83.7%. That's a +24 percentage point improvement! ML shines when there are more bad cookies to avoid.
