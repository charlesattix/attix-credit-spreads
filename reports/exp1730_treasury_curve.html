1. Strategy Overview

Thesis

The US Treasury yield curve mean-reverts on multi-month horizons. Sustained steepness gets flattened by central bank hikes; deep inversions resolve via cuts. We trade two ETF-spread proxies in parallel:

  • 2s10s proxy: log(TLT / SHY) — long end vs front end
  • 5s30s proxy: log(TLT / IEF) — long end vs belly

Signal

  • Rolling 252-day z-score of each log spread
  • Entry: |z| > 2.0 (Wave 2: stricter than Wave 1's 1.5)
  • z > +2.0 → flatten trade (short long-leg, long short-leg)
  • z < -2.0 → steepen trade (long long-leg, short short-leg)
  • Exit: z crosses zero (mean), or 60-day max hold

Sizing & costs

2% notional risk per trade per pair, split equally between the two legs. $0.005/share commission and 2 bps round-trip slippage on each leg, applied at both entry and exit.
