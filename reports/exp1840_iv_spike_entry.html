1. Strategy Logic

Hypothesis

Credit spread sellers are paid to absorb volatility risk. Instead of entering on a fixed cadence (EXP-1220's 7d), time entries to moments when IV spikes above its 5-day rolling mean by ≥ 1.5σ. These moments offer the richest premium.

Signal

  • IV proxy = (ATM put + ATM call) / SPY, from real IronVault front-month prices
  • Rolling 5-day mean and std (causal — excludes today)
  • Spike: z = (today - mean) / std ≥ 1.5
  • Enter bull put spread: sell 3% OTM, buy $5 lower
  • Exit: 50% profit, 2× stop, or 5 DTE
