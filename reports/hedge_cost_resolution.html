Break-even thresholds

  • Collar becomes viable at alpha > 1.81%/yr (current 1.5% — still 0.31% short)
  • Selective puts become viable at alpha > 2.10%/yr (need +0.60% alpha)
  • Continuous puts need alpha > 4.36%/yr (nearly 3x current)
  • At 5%+ alpha, all three options are net positive and collar becomes clearly dominant

6. Methodology

Data sources

  • Real put costs: IronVault 5% OTM SPY puts, 2020-2025 (commit 51e11e6)
  • VIX-based pricing: VIX_TO_PUT_COST table in compass/smart_hedge.py
  • Baseline alpha: EXP-1220 trade-level (1.2-1.5%/yr, commit 1ef262a)
  • Crisis drawdowns: Phase 6 stress test with 1.5x credit spread beta
  • Collar offset: 70% of put cost offset by selling 3% OTM calls

Assumptions

  • Selective VIX<15 strategy: hedge active ~18% of days (empirical), pays VIX-12 to VIX-15 rate (~2.1%)
  • Collar caps upside at 3% OTM calls → ~0.5%/yr opportunity cost during strong rallies
  • Crisis DD reductions are approximate (full backtest in smart_hedge.py tests)
  • Sharpe impact computed as (cost drag / alpha) × baseline Sharpe
