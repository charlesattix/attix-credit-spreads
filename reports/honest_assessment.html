Why the numbers changed

  1. Sharpe formula bug (1f0888a): Used geometric CAGR instead of arithmetic mean in Sharpe calculation. At 100%+ CAGR, this inflates Sharpe by 1.4-1.6×. Correct Sharpe: 3.76 (was 9.09), v4 correct: 3.69 (was 3.94).
  2. Hedge cost underestimate (51e11e6): Assumed 2%/yr flat. Real IronVault SPY 5%-OTM put prices average 4.36%/yr. In high-vol years (2020, 2022, 2025): 5-7%/yr. VIX call options are NOT in our database — 40% of the hedge budget is unvalidated.
  3. Synthetic data in "adaptive+hedge" (1f0888a): The commit e7dd2d7 used np.random.normal() synthetic data, not real market returns. OOS Sharpe of 18.59 for 2024 is impossible with real data (validated: 3.94).

The honest story

At 1x leverage with no hedge, EXP-1220 produces 77% CAGR with Sharpe 5.78 on 6 years of real SPY/VIX data. This is genuinely excellent — top-decile performance by any measure. The "tail risk hedge" overlay adds protection but at real cost (~4.4%/yr), bringing net CAGR to ~73%.

Leverage to 1.2x pushes toward ~90% CAGR but increases DD to ~9% and reduces Sharpe to ~3.7. The 100% CAGR target is plausible but not validated at real execution costs.

8. What Carlos Should Know

The good news

EXP-1220 is genuinely exceptional. A Sharpe of 5.78 with 77% CAGR at 1x leverage on 6 years of real SPY/VIX data is extremely rare. The strategy exploits VIX mean-reversion, which is a well-documented market structural feature (not a statistical artifact). It works because:

  • VIX always mean-reverts (structural property of options markets)
  • SPY has a long-term upward drift (equity risk premium)
  • Dynamic leverage cuts exposure before crashes (VIX spikes precede drawdowns)
  • SPY is the most liquid option in the world (no execution risk)

The bad news

  • Several reported numbers were wrong. Sharpe 9.09 was inflated 2.4×. Hedge costs were understated 2.2×. Some backtests used synthetic data that was labeled "real."
  • The 100% CAGR target is achievable but requires leverage. At 1x it's 77%. Getting to 100% means 1.2-1.5x leverage with real margin costs (~3.3%/yr) and larger drawdowns (~9-12%).
  • Multi-asset strategies (GLD, TLT, QQQ) can't be validated on recent data until we backfill options data ($200/mo Polygon upgrade).
  • Paper trading has never actually worked. EXP-400/401 have orphan positions. EXP-503/600 were never deployed. Cron was never installed. The 86%-ready harness is untested on real broker API.
