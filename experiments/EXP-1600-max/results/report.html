3. Failed Experiments & Lessons Learned

Experiment Hypothesis What Failed Lesson
EXP-1110 Cross-asset signals lead SPY All 6 assets correlated at lag=0 (contemporaneous), overlay hurt WR by -18.6pp Cross-asset momentum is a contrarian signal, not a leading one; use as filter to avoid entry
EXP-1320 Intraday vol clustering improves timing Autocorrelation only 0.126 (target 0.5), overlay hurt WR by -11.1pp Daily data cannot replicate intraday vol clustering; need real 5-min bars for this to work
EXP-1230 Microstructure predicts vol (standalone) AUC 0.49, standalone Sharpe -0.03 Microstructure has zero standalone alpha but massive value as an entry filter (+21pp WR)
EXP-1420 Numpy transformer beats XGBoost 54% accuracy vs 55.9% XGBoost; Sharpe 0.43 vs 1.38 Gradient-free training can't optimize 40K+ parameters; for small datasets, trees dominate neural nets
EXP-980 3.5x leverage feasible at brokers No broker supports 3.5x on options; max realistic is 2.0-3.0x Credit spread margin = max loss, not notional; practical CAGR target is 55-77% at 2.0x
EXP-1020 0-DTE butterfly scalping Only 0.8 trades/month (target 4+), correlation 0.35 (target 0.2) Strategy works (67.8% WR, 2.95 Sharpe) but needs real 1-min 0-DTE data for sufficient frequency
Meta-lesson: The most common failure mode is not that a signal is useless — it's that it lacks standalone predictive power but has high value as an overlay filter. 3 of 6 failed experiments were rescued by pivoting from standalone strategy to EXP-880 overlay. Always test signals both standalone AND as overlays.
