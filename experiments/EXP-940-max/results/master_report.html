Risk Analysis

Tail Risk Mitigation

  • Drawdown-controlled delevering: reduces exposure from 2x to 0.5x as DD hits 2%→7%
  • Kill switch at 8%: automatic full liquidation
  • VIX spike exit: close all positions if VIX jumps >30% in one day
  • Daily loss limit at 3%: prevents compounding intraday losses
  • Crisis hedge: only 0.33%/yr drag, activates automatically

Known Risks

  • Gap risk: overnight moves can’t be hedged; $5 width limits max loss per contract
  • Model degradation: quarterly retraining mitigates; OOS monitoring required
  • Execution risk: bid-ask slippage modeled; limit orders enforced
  • Correlation breakdown: crisis correlations rise to 0.40-0.75; stress-tested
  • Regime misclassification: HMM + ensemble reduces whipsaw by 41%
