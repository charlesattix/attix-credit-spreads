Paper Trading Recommendation

EXP-1220 is an overlay, not a standalone strategy. It does not place trades — it tells you when to reduce size.

How to paper test:
Wire it on top of EXP-400 or EXP-401. Each day, compute the threat level and scale the position sizing:
Green = 100% of normal size | Yellow = 60% | Orange = 30% | Red = 0% (no new trades)

Downside risk of adding it: zero. It only ever reduces exposure, never increases it.
Validation window: Remaining paper trading period through May 11, 2026.
