Paper Trading Assessment

EXP-1220 is a risk overlay, not a standalone strategy. It tells you WHEN to reduce exposure, not WHAT to trade.

How it works: Monitors 5 crash signals (VIX level, VIX term structure, VIX momentum, SPY drawdown, SPY momentum). Outputs daily threat level: green/yellow/orange/red.

Does it need paper trading?

  • Standalone: No — it does not generate trades. It generates a daily threat score.
  • As overlay on EXP-400/401/503/600: YES. Apply as position sizing filter:
    • Green = 100% normal size
    • Yellow = 60%
    • Orange = 30%
    • Red = 0% (no new trades)
  • Compare paper results with vs without overlay through May 11 validation window.

Recommendation: Wire as sizing overlay on EXP-400 or EXP-401. It only reduces size (never increases), so downside risk is zero. Real data shows massive DD reduction in every year tested.
