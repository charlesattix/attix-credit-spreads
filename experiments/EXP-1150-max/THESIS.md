# EXP-1150-max: Calendar Effects Alpha

## Hypothesis
Systematic calendar anomalies provide statistically significant edge in SPY options timing. Combining 8+ effects into a daily calendar score can be used standalone or as a timing overlay for EXP-880.

## Calendar Effects Modeled
1. Turn of Month (ToM): last 2 + first 3 trading days outperform
2. Options Expiration (OpEx): week of monthly expiry has higher vol
3. FOMC Drift: positive drift in 24hrs before FOMC announcement
4. Quad Witching: increased vol around quarterly expiration
5. Santa Rally: last 5 days of Dec + first 2 of Jan
6. Sell in May: May-Oct historically underperforms Nov-Apr
7. Monday Effect: Mondays historically weaker
8. Month-End Rebalancing: pension/index fund flows at month-end
