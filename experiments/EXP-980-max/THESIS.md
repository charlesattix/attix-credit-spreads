# EXP-980-max: Margin & Broker Feasibility Analysis

## Question

EXP-950 showed 3.5x leverage yields 45% CAGR with 10% DD. EXP-960 showed a combined portfolio could reach 100% CAGR. But can we actually *get* 3.5x leverage from a real broker on options strategies?

## Key Issues

1. Reg-T vs Portfolio Margin — which do we qualify for?
2. Credit spread margin = max loss, not notional — how does this map to leverage?
3. Multi-underlying diversification benefit under portfolio margin
4. Worst-case margin expansion during VIX spikes
5. Broker-specific rules (Alpaca vs IBKR vs Tastytrade)
